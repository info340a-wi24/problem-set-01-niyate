Princess Tiana

Princess Tiana is the main character in the movie The Princess and the Frog . She came from working as waitress, in order to save up money to open her new restruant. She kisses a prince (that was turned into a frog) and she becomes a frog as well. The whole movie is a story about them trying to become human again. I admire her because of the following three things.

  • She is hardworking
  • She is kind
  • She is humble
